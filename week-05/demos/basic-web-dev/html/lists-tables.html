Grilled Salmon Recipe

Cooking this grilled salmon is quick, easy and delicious. Spice this up with a variety of sides like brocolli or rice and you have a full meal. Example Final Product

Ingredients

  • A large slab of salmon
  • Avocado Oil
  • Your favorite seasonings
    • Salt
    • Black Pepper
    • Paprika
    • Garlic Powder
    • Onion Powder

Cooking Instructions

  1. On a cutting board you can clean easily, slice the slab of salmon into approximately 8oz fillets
  2. Preheat grill to 400 degrees F
  3. Lightly drizzle oil onto the salmon
  4. Generously sprinkle your favorite seasonings on the salmon
  5. Grill salmon for 12 minutes started skin side down and flipping after 8 minutes
